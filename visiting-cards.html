Everything You Need to Know

Our standard visiting cards are digitally printed on premium 300 GSM paper, ensuring a professional look and feel. They are ideal for networking, personal branding, and daily business use. Easy to design, order, and receive!

Available in both matte and glossy finishes to suit your preference. Our printing process guarantees vibrant colors and sharp details, making your contact information easily readable and memorable.

Detailed Specifications

  • **Card Size:** 3.5 x 2 inches (Standard)
  • **Material Options:** 300 GSM Matte Paper, 300 GSM Glossy Paper, 250 GSM Textured Paper
  • **Print Type:** Single-sided or Double-sided CMYK Printing
  • **Corners:** Sharp or Rounded (3mm radius)
  • **Resolution:** High-resolution (300 DPI recommended for submitted designs)
  • **Bleed Area:** 3mm on all sides recommended
  • **Packaging:** Securely packed to prevent damage

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to print and deliver?

A: Standard orders are typically printed within 2-3 business days and delivered within 4-7 business days, depending on your location.

Q: Can I upload my own design?

A: Yes, absolutely! You can upload your design files (PDF, JPG, PNG) directly on the order page. Please ensure your design is high-resolution (300 DPI).

Q: What if I don't have a design?

A: No problem! We offer professional design services at an additional cost. Contact us with your requirements, and our team will help you create a stunning design.

Q: Do you offer bulk discounts?

A: Yes, discounts are available for larger quantities. The pricing automatically adjusts as you select higher quantities.
